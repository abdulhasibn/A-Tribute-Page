Life events of Muhammad(Peace be Upon Him)

  • He attained Prophethood at the age of 40, at Hira(cave).
  • He preached Monotheism, the Oness of Allah the Almighty, in the city of Mecca for 13 years
  • The people of Mecca, who are none but his own Kinsmen persecuted him for preaching that there is none worthy of worship except Allah and he (peace be upon him) is his Messenger
  • When situation went from bad to worse, he(peace be upon him) decided to migrate to someother place and chose Medina as his new home.
  • The people of medina were welcoming and they accepted Islam
  • They had already pledged allegiance to The Prophet(peace be upon him) at 'Aqaba when some tribesmen from Medina came to mecca for hajj
  • He (peace be upon him) faced several battles during the ten years of Islamic rule in medina
  • After his Death, Aby Bakr, the prophet's(peace be upon him) closest companion succeeded him as The Caliph
